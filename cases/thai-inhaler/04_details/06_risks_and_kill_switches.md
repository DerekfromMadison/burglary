## Phase 0 Kill-Switches

- **Conversion <1.0%** → pivot bundle-toolkit or non-heritage.
- **Conversion 1.0–1.5%** → conditional; another 21-day cycle.
- **AOV <$18** → bundle pull failing.
- **Return >5%** → product issue. >8% = kill.
- **Creator pickup <20%** → authenticity not landing.
- **CAC >$25** → unit economics fail.
- **Subscription opt-in <8%** → LTV collapses.
- **Any compliance event** → auto full-stop. Investigate, fix, restart.
- **3 variants × 4 weeks miss threshold** → messaging fails. Pivot language.

## Phase 1 Kill-Switches

- **No LOI by day 60** → Wedge B immediately. Day 120 no agreement = pivot.
- **Apothékary/Saje inhaler launches** → halt cold, accelerate retention.
- **FDA warning to comparable <6mo** → tighten proactively.
- **90-day repeat <15% month 6** → cut paid 50%, subscription + content.
- **CAC >$40 month 4** → pause paid; Erewhon + creator organic.
