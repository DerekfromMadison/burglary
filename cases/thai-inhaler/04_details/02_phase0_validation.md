# Phase 0: Validation Protocol

**TL;DR.** $5–15k over 6 weeks across 4 parallel tests. Decision gate is a 4-signal AND with above-median thresholds. $5k = directional; $10–15k = 95% confidence.

---

## The Four Tests

### Test 1: Private-Label MVP ($5k base / $10–15k for power)

500 units Poy-Sian Mark II or Hong Thai wholesale (~$1.20/unit) + custom over-sleeve. No formulation change. List Amazon FBA Beauty + Shopify PDP at $14 / $24 / $36. Run $1.5k Meta ads (directional) or $3–5k (statistical).

**Power note.** $5–8 cold CPM × 1% CTR × $1.5k = ~1,500 clicks. 95% CI on 1.5% conversion ±0.5pp needs ~2,300 visitors per arm. $5k tells you "below kill" or "above strong-go" — not "confident conditional."

### Test 2: Fake-PDP Price Test ($3k, parallel weeks 2–4)

Three Meta PDPs at $9.99 / $14.99 / $19.99 × 3 creative variants. Measure CTR + add-to-cart drop-off. Independent price-elasticity signal. The Amazon Beauty referral cliff at $10 (8% under, 15% over) makes $9.99 vs $14.99 structurally important.

### Test 3: FDA Language Audit ($2k, parallel weeks 2–4)

1–2 hours of cosmetic-FDA practice counsel (Greenberg Traurig, Hyman Phelps & McNamara, or equivalent). Audit website headline, top 5 PDP claims, affiliate-creator brief. Buys the difference between live brand and warning-letter death.

### Test 4: Creator Gifting (free, parallel weeks 2–6)

30 gifted inhalers + handwritten card + language card + supplier story to nervous-system + Thai-American micro-creators (5K–50K). Signal: pickup rate. Well-run programs hit 20–40%; <10% = cultural authenticity not landing.

## Decision-Gate Thresholds (all recalibrated above indie-wellness median)

| Metric | Kill | Conditional | Strong-go | Source benchmark |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Cold-ad conversion at $14 | <1.0% | 1.5–2.5% | >2.5% | Health/Wellness Shopify median 1.4–1.8%; cold paid-social 0.8–1.5%; top 10% >4.7%. Median is *not* a green light. |
| AOV | <$18 | $18–22 | ≥$22 | Internally derived from $14/$24/$36 ladder; ≥$22 proves 1-in-3 buyers ladder up. |
| 30-day return rate | >8% | 5–8% | <5% | Beauty/cosmetics ~4.99% (Shippo); supplements 2–5%. <5% matches category baseline. |
| Creator pickup of 30 | <10% | 10–20% | ≥20% | Well-run gifting averages 20–40%. |
| Blended CAC | >$25 | $20–25 | <$20 | Required for $14 single / $24 2-pack unit economics. |
| Subscription opt-in | <5% | 5–8% | ≥8% | LTV math collapses below 8% regardless of conversion. |
| Compliance events | Any | — | Zero | Cease-and-desist, Amazon takedown, platform claim flag = automatic full-stop. |

## Decision Tree (Week 6)

**Strong-go.** ≥3-of-4 primary thresholds at strong-go, FDA audit clean, supplier track on day-60 milestone, zero compliance events → Phase 1 commit.

**Conditional.** Conversion 1.5–2.5% at $14 but starter-set AOV strong-go → Pivot to Adjacent Wedge B (bundle toolkit: inhaler stick + balm + pendant + breathwork audio + ritual cards as $58–84 kit vs. Saje Pocket Farmacy $84).

**No-go.** Conversion in kill OR creator pickup <10% OR any compliance flag → Audience-first pivot. 12 months of newsletter/TikTok content on "daytime nervous-system tools," monetize via Amazon affiliate to existing brands, launch own product year-two at near-zero CAC. Wirecutter/Vox path. Costs $0–5k vs. $50–250k launch.

**Creative-fatigue trigger.** 3 creative variants × 4 weeks miss threshold → wedge messaging is the failure point. Pivot language frame before product.

## What Phase 0 Does NOT Test

Private-label validates *demand for a packaged Thai inhaler at $14* — not fully the wedge's *positioning claim* ("1-second reset / glimmer"). Fake-PDP partially compensates via creative variants. Phase 0 strong-go is necessary, not sufficient. Phase 1 custom-inventory launch is still a bet.
