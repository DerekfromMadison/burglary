# Wave-1: Speed to First 40 Customers + PMF Measurement (v2)

**Case**: thai-inhaler
**Lens**: Speed-to-PMF — the gating metric for everything else
**Date**: 2026-04-27
**Supersedes:** `21_speed_to_100.md`
**Why v2:** v1 targeted 100 customers in 90 days at <$50 blended CAC. The economics-gap report demonstrated cash-only blended CAC was ~$140 (not $50) and the execution-gap report demonstrated the workload required to hit 100 was a 71–90 hr/wk three-person plan. v2 cuts the customer target to **40**, commits to a **single $12 price**, restates **honest CAC math**, and adds a **financial kill-switch block** at Day 45 / 60 / 75 that halts the second $25k tranche if any two trigger.

---

## 1. THE PLAY (one sentence)

**Get to 40 paying customers in 90 days at <$60 blended cash CAC across friends, IRL sampling (1 city, 2 venues), organic TikTok (4×/wk batch-shot), Reddit/Pinterest/LinkedIn organic, and Amazon (IP-Accelerator-unblocked) — then run the Sean Ellis 40% test on those 40 to decide whether the second $25k tranche is rational.**

If the founder buys $1 of paid Meta before customer #30 OR before Day 75 Sean Ellis read, this plan has failed. Paid is Phase 2 (month 4+).

## 2. THE 90-DAY CALENDAR (revised — solo-feasible at ~38–48 hr/wk)

- **Day 2–3**: Build 1-page Shopify at **$12 single SKU only** (no 2-pack, no starter set, no subscription — per `12_beachhead_sku_v2.md`). Discount code `FRIEND25` (25% off, max 30 redemptions). Klaviyo welcome flow draft + Postscript SMS account creation.

- **Day 7**: Draft the friends DM template. Build a list of 30 personal contacts ranked by ICP-fit (women 28–48, post-class wellness adjacent, urban). Don't send yet — wait for inventory.

**Week 1 founder hours: ~14 hrs.**

### Week 2–3 (days 8–21) — First 6–10 customers via friends + IRL

- **Day 10–14**: Inventory arrives (small first batch via air freight if 3PL allows). QC, hand-apply paper sleeves on the kitchen table, hand-number the first 50 cards.

**Week 2–3 founder hours: ~16 hrs/wk.**

### Week 4–6 (days 22–42) — Next 10–15 via TikTok organic + Reddit + Pinterest

**Week 4–6 founder hours: ~17 hrs/wk.**

### Week 7–12 (days 43–90) — Last 12–18 via Amazon launch + lifecycle activation

- **Day 43–60**: Amazon listing matures. Vine reviews begin posting day 8–45 of claim window. PPC at $34/day. Goal: 8–15 Amazon orders by day 90.

- **Day 75–90**: Day-75 Sean Ellis read decides whether second $25k tranche is rational.
- **Running total at day 90**: ~38–45 customers. Spend: ~$13,400.

**Week 7–12 founder hours: ~14 hrs/wk.**

**Total 90-day founder hours: ~38–48 hr/wk** — solo-feasible if no day job (per `gap_execution §5 verdict`).

## 4. THE PMF MEASUREMENT (revised thresholds)

### Sean Ellis 40% test (the primary instrument)

- **Trigger**: Day 21 post-purchase (single-SKU cohort — no bundle-attach math needed because there is no bundle).
- **Survey** (Klaviyo, $5 reorder credit): standard Sean Ellis 4-question battery.
- **Target N**: **≥30 responses by Day 90** (revised down from v1's "N≥40 by Day 60" — realistic post-purchase response rate is ~30%, not 40%).
- **Decision threshold**: ≥40% "very disappointed" = green light to deploy second $25k tranche. 30–40% = iterate positioning, hold the second tranche. <30% = halt second tranche; pivot positioning before any further acquisition.

### Repeat-purchase rate (the behavior instrument)

- **Day-30 repeat**: target ≥15%.
- **Day-60 repeat**: target ≥25%.
- **Day-90 repeat**: target ≥30% (revised down from v1's 35% — the unboxing + 3pm sticker + depletion SMS combination is now the mechanism, not aspiration).

### NPS

- Fire post-Day-30. Target: NPS ≥40.

### Qualitative

- **5 customer interviews by Day 60** (revised down from 10 — solo-feasibility cut).
- One question matters: "Walk me through the last time you used it." If 4 of 5 describe the post-class lobby OR the 3pm sticker triggering an inhale, the wedge is real.

## 5. KILL / ITERATE CRITERIA (the financial kill-switch block — NEW)

Hard numeric triggers, codified. Two trips = halt the second $25k tranche.

| Trigger | Threshold | Action if fires |
|---|---|---|
| **Day 45 — cash CAC** | >$100 by Day 45 | Pause IRL + creator gifting; reroute remaining budget to the single highest-converting channel (likely friends + Reddit if both still pulling). |
| **Day 60 — inventory sell-through** | <20% of 1,500 units (i.e., <300 sold) | Halt second OEM order. ICP wrong, not supply. |
| **Day 60 — 30-day repeat purchase rate** | <8% | Aromatherapy-replenishment thesis dead. Shift remaining Y1 budget from acquisition to retention experiments (refill-first SMS, ritual-card overhaul, cohort-program pilot). |
| **Day 75 — Sean Ellis "very disappointed"** | <30% on N≥30 | Stop all paid spend. Pivot positioning. Re-survey at Day 120. |

**Combined trip rule**: if any 2 of the 4 fire, the founder DOES NOT spend the second-90-day $25k tranche. Burn the lesson, not the budget. (Per `gap_economics §5`.)

Additional triggers carried forward from v1:
- **FDA warning letter to a comparable brand within 90 days**: tighten claim language proactively. Audit all creator content.
- **Founder cannot secure Thai supplier-equity by Day 60**: NOT a kill-switch in v6 (was in v1). Supplier-equity is now Y2 work per `14_differentiation_v2.md`.
