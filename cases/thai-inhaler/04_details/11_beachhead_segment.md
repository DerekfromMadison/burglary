# Wave-1 Beachhead Segment — thai-inhaler

**Author**: Beachhead segment selector (Wave-1 marketing strategy sub-agent)
**Date**: 2026-04-27
**Constraints**: Bootstrap ≤$50k Y1 marketing. Solo founder, phone-led ops. Greenfield brand and audience. IP/TM deferred. Camphor drop = formulation footnote, not strategy. Marketing first. Speed to first 100 paying customers > brand polish.

---

## 1. The recommendation

**Pick the post-Pilates / post-reformer woman, 28–42, in three zip-code clusters: West LA (90402, 90403, 90291), Williamsburg + Park Slope (11211, 11215), and Austin (78704, 78703).** This is a tight, observable slice of the "anxious-Millennial knowledge worker" segment — the only slice that combines Tier-3 wellness WTP, daily physical co-location in a single retail-shaped space (the studio lobby), and a literal post-exertion parasympathetic-crash moment a 5-second inhaler legibly serves.

## 2. Why this is the bet

She is the only segment that supports the $24–$36 price ladder our unit economics require — she pays $40/class three to five times a week ([Pink Pilates Club LA](https://pinkpilatesclub.com/best-pilates-studios-los-angeles/)) and already buys Saje Peppermint Halo at $36 (per A_cmo Tier-3 evidence). The mom-of-young-kids needs Amazon Prime + Target shelf trust we cannot earn in 60 days; the biohacker is the FDA / FTC affiliate-channel landmine that has already buried MONQ, Young Living, and doTERRA distributors (E_final ¶6); the AAPI heritage buyer is a PR shield not a wallet (~330k Thai-Americans nationally, knows the $1 reference price, will not pay $24); the meditation-app drop-out and pre-meeting exec are real but reach through the same nervous-system creator graph at lower density than the Pilates lobby. Critically, this cut also defuses the founder-identity risk surfaced in `decisions.md`: the customer is hiring the product for *post-class breath reset*, not for *Thai heritage*, so Thai-ness becomes a sourcing detail (Wong-family-in-Chiang-Rai, per A_customer Persona C), which lets the founder ship before the supplier-equity partnership is fully cooked. The Pilates lobby is the cheapest credible distribution surface a non-Thai bootstrap founder has — and "hand a real product to 30 women in person per studio per week" beats any TikTok funnel a $50k budget can buy in months 1–3 (and TikTok-only attempts are precisely where saturated-aromatherapy brands die — see [Dazed on the saturated nervous-system content arms race](https://www.dazeddigital.com/beauty/article/69413/1/everyone-trying-regulate-their-nervous-system-tapping-tiktok-meditation)).

## 3. The play (numbered, $50k cap, phone-operable)

**1. Validation inventory + studio hand-sell — $1,000, week 1.** Buy 600 units of Poy-Sian Mark II at $1.17/unit ($702) from [Authentic Muay Thai Supply](https://authenticmuaythaisupply.com/products/poy-sian-mark-ii-menthol-nasal-inhaler) as the validation SKU. Re-label only after validation. Walk into 6 LA studios in week 1 (Forma West Hollywood + Brentwood, Hot Pilates by Bailey Brown, Pilates Plus West Hollywood, Be Kind, Sculpt House, Heimat). Pitch the front-desk manager: free 12-unit tester, teach staff the "post-class 5-second reset," hand-sell at $24 with 50% margin to the studio. Target: 3 studios say yes. Output: 30–60 hand-sold units in weeks 2–4, real PMF signal (does she come back next week and ask for it?).

**2. Pilates-instructor micro-creator drop — $2,500, weeks 2–6.** Ship 30 packages to instructor-creators (5K–80K followers, #pilatesgirlie, #reformerpilates, #vagusnerve crossover) across LA / NYC / Austin. Inventory $50, fulfillment $200, $2,250 reserved for 4–6 paid follow-up posts at $400–$600 to the highest-organic-engagement seeded creators. Brief: "post-class 5-second reset, in your tote, name the smell, no medical claims" — verb-object rule from E_final §H taped to the founder's monitor. Expected output: 6–10 organic posts, 2–4 paid, 1–3M cumulative views, 400–800 trackable PDP visits.

**3. Three-city studio rollout — $4,500, weeks 5–10.** Add Williamsburg / Park Slope (East River Pilates, Sculpt Society SoHo, Bodyrok) + Austin (LifeTime South reformer floor, Castle Hill, Pure Pilates ATX). Founder flies once per city ($1,500 travel each), stays 2 nights, walks 4–6 doors in person. By end of week 10: 8–12 studio doors live, 250–400 hand-sold units, ≥3 doors on unprompted reorder.

**4. DTC site + Klaviyo — $400 + ongoing $0, week 2.** Shopify single SKU at $24, 3-pack at $58. No subscription until reorder data exists. Klaviyo free tier. Studio front-desk captures email + zip code on a clipboard — that list is the entire growth loop in months 1–3. Friend-tells-friend in the lobby is the cheapest CAC dollar in CPG.

**5. Reserve — $41.6k.** $5k Phase 0 fake-PDP price test (E_final §C), $2k FDA language audit (E_final §D), $14k Amazon launch + Vine + 90-day PPC reserved for week 12+ once validation lands (E_final ¶9), $20k contingency / double-down on whichever of (1)-(3) returns best CAC. Do NOT spend the reserve before week 8.

## 4. Success metric

**100 paying customers within 90 days of first studio drop, blended CAC ≤$12, ≥30% reorder rate within 60 days of first purchase, ≥3 of 6 LA studios reordering unprompted.** Sub-thresholds: AOV ≥$24, 30-day return <8%. Kill-switch: if blended CAC >$25 or reorder <15% by day 90, the beachhead is wrong — pivot to E_final Phase 0 alternatives (bundle-format toolkit or Functional-segment via TikTok Shop) without re-launching the brand.

## 5. One thing this week

**Walk into Forma Pilates West Hollywood with 12 Poy-Sian units, a 1-page hand-sell script for the "post-class 5-second reset," and ask the front-desk lead for a 48-hour staff trial.** One door, one conversation, this week. If yes, replicate at 5 more LA studios next week. If no, you've learned a $0 lesson about the studio-channel hypothesis before spending another dollar. The founder's phone, a clipboard for emails, and a parking-meter quarter — that is the entire week-1 stack.
