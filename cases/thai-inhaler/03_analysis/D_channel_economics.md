# D — Channel break-even economics & first-$50k allocation (Round D)

**Case:** thai-inhaler. **Lens:** per-channel P&L + $50K allocation. **Date:** 2026-04-27. **Inputs:** B_synthesis, A_cfo, A_competitor, 01_scope; 2026 web research on Amazon, TikTok Shop, WFM/Erewhon/Sprouts terms, Meta/TikTok CPM/CPC, indie CAC, subscription churn.

## 1. Cost-stack baseline (confirm / update)

CFO's $1.60 blended landed COGS holds, with two updates:

1. **Amazon ended FBA Prep Services Jan 1, 2026** ([AMZ Prep](https://amzprep.com/amazon-fba-fees/)). $0.30 prep moves to 3PL — same cost, different vendor.
2. **Thai 19% reciprocal tariff at $0.143–0.20/unit.** Use $0.17 midpoint.

**Single-tube landed COGS, Y1 (~15K u):** FOB Thailand $0.75 + ocean+clearance $0.10 + 19% tariff $0.17 + 3PL receiving $0.08 + US relabel $0.15 + FBA prep at 3PL $0.30 + 3% shrink $0.05 = **$1.60**.

**Bundle COGS:** 2-pack = 2 × $1.60 + $0.45 sleeve = **$3.65**. 3-pack starter (card + pouch) = 3 × $1.60 + $1.50 = **$6.30**. These feed every channel below.

## 2. Channel-by-channel P&L

### 2.1 Amazon (FBA, Beauty category)

**2026 fees** ([Amazon 2026](https://sellingpartners.aboutamazon.com/update-to-u-s-referral-and-fulfillment-by-amazon-fees-for-2026), [Brandwoven](https://gobrandwoven.com/resources/articles/amazon-2026-fees-breakdown-fba-referral-inbound-placement/)): Beauty referral **8% ≤$10 / 15% >$10** (hard cliff — $9.99 saves 7pp vs. $10.01). Small-standard ≤6oz FBA pick-pack-ship **$3.22–3.47** (Jan 15 increase). Storage $0.78/cuft Jan–Sep, $2.40 Oct–Dec. Inbound placement $0.20–0.45/unit.

| SKU | MSRP | Referral | FBA fulfill | Inbound | COGS | **Net contribution** | **CM%** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Single tube | $9.99 | $0.80 (8%) | $3.22 | $0.30 | $1.60 | **$4.07** | **41%** |
| Single tube | $14.00 | $2.10 (15%) | $3.22 | $0.30 | $1.60 | **$6.78** | **48%** |
| 2-pack | $24.00 | $3.60 (15%) | $3.47 | $0.40 | $3.65 | **$12.88** | **54%** |
| 3-pack starter | $36.00 | $5.40 (15%) | $3.85 | $0.45 | $6.30 | **$20.00** | **56%** |

**WC:** Amazon pays every 14d (~$3K receivables on a $50K month). Target 6× turn.

**CAC ceiling:** Sponsored Products $8–25/order at launch, declining with organic rank. 2-pack $12.88 contribution → CAC ceiling ~$13 break-even / ~$16 with 25% 90d repeat. **Cheapest deployable cold-buyer CAC** — traffic is already in purchase intent.

### 2.2 DTC website (Shopify)

Fees: 2.9% + $0.30 processing; $5–8 outbound ship (free over $30); $1 pack; $1 pick at 3PL.

| SKU | MSRP | Processing | Ship | Pack/pick | COGS | **Net** | **CM%** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Single $14 | $14.00 | $0.71 | $5.00 (paid by buyer) | $1.00 | $1.60 | **$10.69** | **76%** |
| 2-pack $24 | $24.00 | $1.00 | free (absorb $6) | $1.00 | $3.65 | **$12.35** | **52%** |
| 3-pack $36 | $36.00 | $1.34 | free (absorb $7) | $1.00 | $6.30 | **$20.36** | **57%** |
| 3-pack subscription (15% off) | $30.60 | $1.19 | free | $1.00 | $6.30 | **$16.11** | **53%** |

**Subscription bonus:** replenishment churn 7–10%/mo ([Recharge](https://getrecharge.com/blog/10-subscription-metrics-every-dtc-brand-should-track/)) → ~8-mo life. $16 × 8 = **$128 LTV**, 4× one-shot. Annual prepay cuts churn 51%; push annual.

**CAC ceilings:** one-shot $36 starter w/ 25% 90d repeat: 90d value $25, **CAC ≤ $25 break-even**. Subscription $128 LTV: **CAC ≤ $60 break-even, ≤ $42 hits 3:1**. This is where the math works.

Indie-wellness paid CAC $89 supplements / $148 H&B ([MHI 2026](https://mhigrowthengine.com/blog/average-cost-per-acquisition-by-dtc-vertical-2026/)). One-shot DTC is uneconomic at category CAC; subscription conversion is the gate.

### 2.3 TikTok Shop

**2026 fees** ([Dashboardly](https://www.dashboardly.io/post/tiktok-shop-fees-2026-the-complete-seller-fee-guide), [OneCart](https://www.getonecart.com/tiktok-shop-seller-fees/)): US referral **6%** (down from 8%); new-seller 1.8–3% first 30–90d. Affiliate beauty/wellness commission **15–30%, 20–25% wins**. TFS or self-ship ~$5. Creator-driven beauty sale: 35–40% of GMV in platform costs before COGS.

| SKU | MSRP | Referral 6% | Affiliate 25% | Ship/pack | COGS | **Net** | **CM%** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 2-pack $24 | $24.00 | $1.44 | $6.00 | $5.00 | $3.65 | **$7.91** | **33%** |
| 3-pack $36 | $36.00 | $2.16 | $9.00 | $5.00 | $6.30 | **$13.54** | **38%** |

**No-affiliate organic Shop button:** 3-pack contribution rises to $22.54 (63%). Owned-vs-paid creator swings channel from healthy to uneconomic.

**CAC ceiling:** TikTok CPC $0.40–1.20, CPM $4.50–9.16 ([Lebesgue 2026](https://lebesgue.io/tiktok-ads/tiktok-ads-benchmarks-for-ctr-cr-and-cpm)); cold Shop CR 1–2% → paid CAC $30–80. **At $13.54 net per affiliate-driven 3-pack, paid TikTok loses money on order one.** Viable only on organic creator hits; post-USDS compression (B_synthesis #6) makes the 2024 playbook riskier.

### 2.4 Whole Foods (regional, then national)

**Terms** ([GoVividly](https://www.govividly.com/blog/working-with-whole-foods-market-a-guide-for-cpg-brands), [Grocery Nerd](https://grocerynerd.substack.com/p/grocery-update20-retailer-and-wholesaler), [Retail Wire](https://retailwire.com/discussion/whole-foods-asks-vendors-to-pay-to-play/)): **~50% off MSRP**. No formal slotting; **1 case free-fill / SKU / store** + MCBs. **Pay-to-play promo contributions** increasingly required post-2024 centralization. Net 30-60. Demos $200–500/store/mo. WFM LEAP program for emerging brands.

| SKU | MSRP | Wholesale (50% off) | Free-fill amortized over 12 mo | COGS | **Net per unit** | **CM%** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Single $14 | $14.00 | $7.00 | -$0.40 | $1.60 | **$5.00** | **36%** |
| 2-pack $24 | $24.00 | $12.00 | -$0.55 | $3.65 | **$7.80** | **33%** |
| 3-pack $36 | $36.00 | $18.00 | -$0.70 | $6.30 | **$11.00** | **31%** |

**Cost to enter regional WFM (1 region, ~80 stores):** Free-fill 12u × 80 = $1,540 COGS; LEAP onboarding ~$500; demos $2,880 (6 months); first PO 960u × $7 = $6,720 receivable, Net 30-60 (~$3.5K cash drag). **All-in regional WFM trial: $5–10K.** Net 60 + free-fill = ~$15K stuck in WC for an 80-store pilot.

**CAC ceiling:** Theoretical $0 (retailer brings traffic). Practically $200–500/store/month demos. At 18u/store/mo sell-through, **~$1.50/customer** cost-per-trial. **Cheapest CAC channel by an order of magnitude.**

Rigorous vetting (spec sheets, sourcing docs). Inclusion fees for raw ingredients; demo investment + merchandising for finished goods. Demos $200–600/store/mo. **Volume reality:** 8–25 units/month sustained, 30–80/mo at peak virality. Erewhon is **PR ROI, not revenue ROI.** $5K → 100 units sold = $50 CPU, but earned press is worth 10× that in cross-channel CAC reduction.

## 3. The break-even comparison table

| Channel | First-order CM ($) | First-order CM% | CAC ceiling (1-shot) | CAC ceiling w/ 25% 90d repeat | Working-capital drag | Time to first $ |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Amazon** (2-pack) | $12.88 | 54% | $13 | $16 | Low (14d payment) | 4–6 wks |
| **DTC one-shot** (3-pack) | $20.36 | 57% | $20 | $25 | None (paid up front) | 1 wk |
| **DTC subscription** (3-pack at 8mo lifetime) | LTV $128 | 53%/order | $42 healthy / $60 break-even | n/a | None | 1 wk |
| **TikTok Shop** (3-pack, w/ 25% affiliate) | $13.54 | 38% | $13 | $17 | Low (7d payment) | 2–3 wks |
| **Whole Foods regional** | $7.80 (2-pack) | 33% | ~$1.50/buyer (foot traffic) | n/a | High (Net 60 + free-fill) | 6–9 mo |
| **Erewhon halo** | $9.55 (2-pack) | 40% | ~$50 effective (PR ROI) | n/a | Low | 3–6 mo |
| **Sprouts** | $7.80 (2-pack) | 33% | ~$2/buyer | n/a | High | 9–12 mo |
| **Indie boutiques** | $11.70 (3-pack) | 33% | low (PR + word-of-mouth) | n/a | Medium | 4–8 wks |

**Cheapest CAC:** Whole Foods (foot traffic), but with high fixed cost-of-entry; **cheapest *deployable* CAC at $50K scale: Amazon.**

**Fastest break-even per customer:** DTC subscription ($128 LTV vs. $35 CAC, ~3 orders).

**Highest LTV:** DTC subscription, then Erewhon halo (premium cross-shop behavior).

**Deepest CAC ceiling:** DTC subscription ($60 break-even); Amazon/one-shot DTC/TikTok at $13–25 — far below the $89–148 indie-wellness blended CAC. **The bundle + subscription is the only configuration that survives current channel CAC.**

## 4. The first-$50k allocation

Bootstrap founder, no outside capital, 12-month runway. Inventory ($14K, 9K units) is covered separately per CFO; this $50K is marketing + brand + retail trial.

| Line | $ | What it buys | KPI / output |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1. Compliance + IP** | $5,000 | FDA cosmetic-claims atty review, trademark, LLC + insurance, MoCRA registration | Claims FDA-cleared; brand name cleared; PL insurance. Non-negotiable. |
| **2. Amazon launch** | **$14,000** | $4K listing (A+, 7 photos, lifestyle video, prep); $2K Vine + brand registry; **$8K PPC** over 90d on 8 Thai/sensory keywords | BSR top 50 Beauty > Inhalers in 90d. 200–400 first-90d units. CAC $13–18. ROAS 3.5× by day 60. |
| **3. Creator seeding** | $9,000 | 50 micro at $180 avg, 5 macro at $0–500 (gifted), 1–2 paid Thai-American/nervous-system creators at $1,500 | 30+ UGC clips, 5+ creators >50K views, 200–500 DTC orders. Effective CAC $18–30. |
| **4. Erewhon (1 door)** | **$6,000** | Buyer pitch + samples ($800), PO float ($300), 12 weekly demos × $400 ($4,800), merchandising ($100) | 1 door live; 60–150 units / 90d; "Erewhon-stocked" PR; organic IG/TikTok lift. Press multiplier. |
| **5. Indie boutiques** | $2,500 | Mailers to 30 boutiques (Pop Up Grocer, Cap Beauty, Detox Market, regional spas); first POs to 8 | 6–10 placements; halo PR; HENRY-circle word-of-mouth. |
| **6. Fake-PDP + paid-social test** | $5,000 | $1.5K creative (3 variants × 2 audiences × 2 hooks); $3.5K Meta + TikTok over 21d against 3 positioning hypotheses | Validated angle ≤ $2 CPC, ≤ $30 CPI. Output is signal, not orders. Resolves cold-ad CR unknown. |
| **7. Brand-asset shoot** | $4,000 | 1-day shoot: 30 stills, 8 lifestyle, 3 ASMR videos, founder B-roll | 18-month content runway. |
| **8. PR + sampler kits** | $2,500 | 80 mailers (Cut, Goop, Cup of Jo, Allure, Well+Good, Strategist) + 20 founder-narrative outlets | 2–4 Q1 placements; Strategist gift-guide is highest-leverage hit. |
| **9. Founder reserve** | $2,000 | Returned pallet, damages, legal letter, missed ETA | Optionality. |

**Total: $50,000.**

**Single largest line: Amazon launch ($14K, 28%)** — cheapest deployable CAC, ranking compounds, and the Beauty 8% referral cliff at $10 means a $9.99 single + $24 2-pack configuration is structurally favorable.

**Conspicuously absent (intentional):** broad Meta prospecting, podcast sponsorships, packaging redesign, Sephora pitch, PR retainer, TikTok Shop affiliate at scale.

Order of priorities doesn't change (Amazon + creator + 1 retail halo + signal test first; paid social and retail expansion gated on validation). Depth changes, and **subscription infrastructure** becomes affordable. **Subscription is the only configuration where DTC paid CAC math closes against $89–148 category CAC** — if seed money doesn't buy a subscription engine, it buys a bigger losing P&L.

## 6. The "Don't" list — tempting but uneconomic

1. **Don't pitch Sephora, Ulta, CVS, Walgreens, Target, Costco in Y1.** Saje owns Ulta; Vicks owns CVS. Either out-spent on co-op or pattern-matched 6 inches from a $6.78 Vicks (A_competitor 2.2). Single most common indie-wellness money sink.
2. **Don't run broad Meta prospecting before creative validation.** Indie-wellness CAC $89–148 vs. Amazon $13–18 and DTC subscription break-even $60. Until the $5K fake-PDP validates an angle, every Meta dollar burns.
3. **Don't pay for podcasts pre-traction.** $7K mid-tier × 0.7% conversion = 70 customers at $100 CAC. Worse than every other channel. Podcasts work after $100K revenue, not before.
4. **Don't redesign packaging before 1,000 units sell through.** Behavior data justifies redesign, not Slack feedback. The $4K brand-asset shoot makes existing packaging beautiful in content.
5. **Don't fund TikTok Shop affiliate at scale pre-PMF.** 25% commission + 6% referral + ship leaves $7.91 on a $24 2-pack — paid CAC $30+ = $22 loss per order. Deploy affiliate only as retention layer on already-converting organic creator hits.
6. **Don't enter Sprouts Y1.** Same WFM economics, weaker halo. Y2 volume play.
7. **Don't pay for Erewhon smoothie collabs / $25K celebrity stunts.** Hailey Bieber math doesn't apply to a $50K budget. Earn trial first.
8. **Don't hire a fractional CMO or PR retainer pre-product.** $5–8K/month retainers consume the budget; founder-led PR outperforms until $1M ARR.

## Bottom line

Sequence: **compliance → Amazon ranking + creator seeding → 1 Erewhon door → fake-PDP signal → subscription engine post-seed.** Cheapest theoretical CAC is Whole Foods; cheapest deployable CAC at $50K is Amazon. The $14K Amazon line is 28% of the budget because it's the only channel where ranking compounds.

**Y1 output: ~$130K revenue, validated angle, 1 retail halo, 200–400 90-day customers, and the diagnostic data (90d repeat ≥ 22%, blended CAC ≤ $40) that determines fundability.** Right output for $50K.
