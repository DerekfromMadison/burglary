# thai-inhaler — Deliverable v5 (marketing-strategy first)

v5 supersedes v1–v4. Prior versions led with regulatory and IP work; this one leads with the actual founder ask: **a multi-prong marketing strategy with core messaging, beachhead picks, and a 90-day path to first 100 paying customers.** Camphor and IP are footnotes, not headlines.

## The strategy in one sentence

Take the existing $4 Thai-inhaler format, sell it at $12 with one named scent and a paper sleeve to post-Pilates / post-reformer women 28–42 in three named neighborhoods (West LA, Williamsburg/Park Slope, Austin 78704), via in-person studio sampling + ASMR-and-founder-POV TikTok + r/aromatherapy long-form + a founder LinkedIn series — and get to 100 paying customers in ≤90 days at <$50 blended CAC.

The single repeatable customer-facing line: *"The 1-second daytime focus reset — Thai herbal craft, modern American ritual, in your pocket like a pen."*

## The 90-day plan to 100 paying customers (~$16k of $50k Y1 budget)

**Weeks 1–2 — Setup + first 15 customers ($2k)**
- Source 1,500 OEM blank-label units (~$1.85 landed = ~$3,200 production capital), paper-sleeve via Sticker Mule, ship to founder warehouse / FBM.
- Founder LinkedIn post: "I asked LinkedIn for business problems — here's what I learned launching a Thai herbal inhaler brand." Practitioner voice. Wed 8:15am ET.
- 10–30 friends-of-founder DMs with discount code → 15–20 customers at $0 CAC.

**Weeks 3–6 — IRL sampling sprint + first 40 ($4k)**
- 8–12 independent coffee shops, yoga / Pilates / reformer studios in 1 of 3 beachhead cities (founder picks).
- Tethered counter-display sniffer + 1ml take-home keychain mini ($2.50–4 per engaged prospect). QR → SMS opt-in → 20% off code.
- 5 gifted samples to nervous-system / Thai-American / ASMR creators (5–50K followers each).
- Expected: 120–240 scans, 6–18 first orders.

**Weeks 5–8 — TikTok organic + community ($1k)**
- Founder POV "desk drawer reset" videos, daily phone-shot. ASMR creator post in week 4. Thai-American micro-creator content week 6.
- r/aromatherapy long-form (1,500 words, 3-brand side-by-side, primary-source, no brand link). r/herbalism credibility lurking.
- Expected: first 1k video views Day 5–14; community-driven 25 customers by Week 8.

**Weeks 8–12 — Amazon launch + first paid ($9k)**
- Brand Registry approval (filed Week 1) lands.
- Listing live with refillable cutaway main image, $14 vs. $4 Poy-Sian justification stack. Vine ($200 for 18–22 reviews on the 2-pack ASIN).
- First paid: ONLY if (a) organic conversion rate ≥1.5%, (b) AOV ≥$22, (c) 1+ creator clip ≥50K views with ≥3% save rate. Else hold.
- 12-cell test at $5k / 21 days; kill at $400 spend / $60 CPA per cell.
- Expected: 25–35 customers via Amazon + paid.

**Day 21 of every customer cohort**: Sean Ellis 40% test ("how would you feel if you couldn't get this anymore?") via Klaviyo with $5 reorder credit. Target N≥40 by day 60. Decision rule: ≥40% "very disappointed" = double down; <30% = pivot positioning before scaling.

## Core messaging

**Brand promise**: *the next minute brighter*
**Hero claim**: *One inhale. One bright minute. A Thai pocket ritual.*
**Tagline shortlist (ranked)**:
1. "The 1-second pocket reset."
2. "Thai herbal craft. Modern American ritual."
3. "Your morning glimmer, in a tube."

**Voice / tone**: practitioner, not vendor. First-person founder. Specific, sensory, tactile. NEVER: "wellness," "calm," "clean," "soothes," "supports," "balances," "regulates" + body-system. The verb-object cosmetic-safe rule (`04_details/04_fda_compliance_v2.md`) governs every published word.

## PMF measurement (the gating decision)

- **Sean Ellis 40% test** at Day 21 post-purchase (target N≥40 by Day 60).
- **30-day repeat purchase rate** ≥15% (target ≥30% to scale paid).
- **NPS** ≥40.
- **5 qualitative customer interviews** at Day 30.
- **Decision rule**: ≥40% Sean Ellis + ≥30% 30-day repeat = strong-go (scale paid). <30% Sean Ellis = pivot positioning before any further spend.

## What's deliberately NOT in v5

These were headline items in v1–v4 that v5 demotes to footnotes or future work:

- **Camphor reformulation**: 2-week formulation tweak. Not strategic. The OEM gives you a stock formula; ask for "no camphor." Done. (Detail: `04_details/04_fda_compliance_v2.md`.)
- **USPTO trademark / Brand Registry**: file the trademark in Week 1 ($300 self-serve), enroll Brand Registry in Week 2. Both are checkbox tasks, not strategy. (Detail: `04_details/04_fda_compliance_v2.md`.)
- **Supplier-equity negotiation**: 9–12 month parallel track. Phase 1 launches off OEM blank-label; the strategic supplier relationship is Y2 work. (Detail: `04_details/08_supplier_strategy.md`.)
- **Founder-identity bifurcation**: still real, but downstream of marketing strategy, not upstream. (Detail: `04_details/03_founder_bifurcation.md`.)
