# Gap Analysis — Execution Feasibility (Solo Bootstrap Founder)

**Lens**: Can a solo bootstrap founder execute the v5 deliverable + 14 marketing sub-docs in 90 days while running their life?
**Date**: 2026-04-27
**Source files audited**: `04_deliverable_v5.md`, `15_tiktok_playbook.md`, `16_community_playbook.md`, `17_seo_playbook.md`, `19_amazon_playbook.md`, `20_inperson_playbook.md`, `21_speed_to_100.md`, `22_founder_channel.md`.

## 1. Founder-hours arithmetic

The plan as written is a full-time job and a half. Honest accounting of weekly hours, drawn directly from each playbook's stated cadence:

| Playbook | Weekly activity | Hours/wk |
|---|---|---|
| **22_founder_channel.md** | 4 LinkedIn posts (1.5 hr each = drafting, photo, replies) + 3 TikTok (0.5 hr each shot, 0.5 hr captioning) + 1 Substack essay biweekly (4 hr) + X reply-thread daily (0.5 hr × 7) + 3 cold podcast pitches/week (1 hr) | **15–18** |
| **15_tiktok_playbook.md** | Daily founder POV video (0.5 hr shoot + 0.5 hr edit + 0.5 hr publish/comment-reply) × 7 + creator-seed pack-and-ship (5 packages/wk × 0.5 hr) | **12–14** |
| **16_community_playbook.md** | Reddit comments across 8 communities (lurking, drafting, replying, ~1 hr/sub × 4 active subs) + 1 anchor post 1–2/wk (4 hr) + Substack writer outreach (8–12 hr/mo = 2–3 hr/wk) + Discord body-doubling | **8–10** |
| **17_seo_playbook.md** | One 1,500-word pillar article every 6 days (8–10 hr writing + image + schema) + GSC/Plausible weekly check + Substack-writer link outreach | **9–11** |
| **19_amazon_playbook.md** | Helium 10 weekly search-term harvest + Cerebro pulls + bid management + listing iteration + Vine/Insiders ops + Request-a-Review send + review-monitoring | **5–7** |
| **20_inperson_playbook.md** | Stated 8–12 hr/wk (2 weekday afternoons + 1 weekend morning) for venue visits, sniffer restocks, sampling at studios | **10–12** |
| **21_speed_to_100.md** | Sean Ellis survey ops + 10 customer interviews by day 60 (20 min × 10 = 3.5 hr in cohort, plus recruiting + scheduling) + Klaviyo flow tuning + cohort tracking + kill-criteria reading | **4–6** |

**Realistic total: 71–90 founder-hours per week.**

This is *before* sleep, partner relationship, exercise, errands, or the day job most bootstrap founders still hold. The plan implicitly assumes a 12-hour-day, 6-day-week founder with no dependents, no W-2 income, and no existing illness. It is a >70 hr/wk plan dressed as a "solo bootstrap" plan.

## 2. Top 5 execution risks

### Risk 1 — Daily TikTok posting + daily Reddit/community presence is unsustainable past week 6
**Severity: KILL.** Founder content quality collapses around week 4–6 of any daily-posting regime (well-documented across creator burnout literature). Once the founder skips 3 days, the algorithm punishes the account, and the playbook's "Day 5–14 first 1k views" gate slips to Day 30+ — at which point `15_tiktok_playbook.md` instructs the founder to assume shadow-ban and "verify the account," masking the real cause (intermittent posting).
**Mitigation**: Cut TikTok cadence to 4×/week (M/W/F/Sat) and batch-shoot 8 videos every other Saturday. Accept the algorithm haircut. This recovers ~6 hr/wk.

### Risk 2 — IRL sampling + venue management requires a city the founder physically lives in AND time-of-day overlap with studio classes
**Severity: KILL.** `20_inperson_playbook.md` assumes founder lives in Williamsburg/West-LA/78704, can be at a yoga studio at 11am Saturday and 6pm Tuesday, and has 2 weekday afternoons free. If the founder has a day job (highly likely for a bootstrap operator), the 8–12 hr/wk in-person allocation simply does not exist.
**Mitigation**: Pick ONE neighborhood within 2 miles of home; stage all sampling at 1 venue, not 8–12. Accept slower customer trickle. Solo founder cannot manage 12 try-stations.

### Risk 3 — Amazon Brand Registry timeline collides with 90-day gate
**Severity: DELAY.** `19_amazon_playbook.md` states Brand Registry is 1–2 weeks "with trademark in hand" but USPTO trademarks take 8–12 months. v5 lists "USPTO trademark" as a Week-1 checkbox task, which is wrong — the trademark exists at filing but registration (required for full Brand Registry, A+ Content, Vine) lands far past day 90. This means the entire Day 60–90 Amazon push (Vine, A+ Content, Sponsored Brands) is gated by something the founder cannot accelerate.
**Mitigation**: Use Amazon's IP Accelerator program ($300–700) for fast-track Brand Registry on a pending trademark — gets registry in 2–3 weeks not 12 months. v5 must call this out explicitly.

### Risk 4 — Customer interviews + Sean Ellis at scale require recruiting infrastructure the playbook waves away
**Severity: DELAY.** `21_speed_to_100.md` requires 10 founder-led 20-min customer interviews by day 60 plus N≥40 Sean Ellis responses by day 60. A 40% Klaviyo response rate on a $5 reorder credit is aspirational; real-world post-purchase survey response is 8–15%. To get N=40, the founder needs ~300 customers, not 100. The PMF gate is structurally broken for a 100-customer cohort.
**Mitigation**: Lower threshold to N≥20, double-incentivize ($15 credit), and run interviews concurrent with ship-out (day 7 phone call, not day 21 survey). Or extend PMF read to day 120.

### Risk 5 — Founder LinkedIn series compounds slowly; the v5 plan treats it as a 90-day acquisition driver
**Severity: DELAY.** `22_founder_channel.md` honestly forecasts 500 LinkedIn followers at day 30 and 2,000 at day 90 — a base small enough that "founder-channel-attributed first orders" of 15% of Phase-0 traffic is a tiny absolute number (3–8 customers). The deliverable v5's mention of LinkedIn as the "moat" assumes 2-year compounding; it cannot deliver 90-day customers at scale.
**Mitigation**: Reframe LinkedIn as the journalist/buyer/investor channel, NOT the customer channel. Drop expectation that LinkedIn drives the first 100 customers; expect 5–15.

## 3. Hidden prerequisite skills

Skills the playbooks silently assume the founder already has:

1. **Video editing, on-camera presence, scent-vocabulary articulation.** `15_tiktok_playbook.md` requires 30 founder-POV videos in 30 days with sub-1.5-second hooks. Most bootstrap founders cannot deliver this; on-camera fluency is the #1 unstated gating skill.
2. **Long-form writing at journalist quality.** `17_seo_playbook.md` and `22_founder_channel.md` together demand 5 pillar articles (1,500 words each) + 16 LinkedIn posts/month + 2 Substack essays/month = roughly the output of a working journalist or content writer. AI-assist is allowed but the post-edit "make it sound like a human who held the product" is itself a skilled craft.
3. **Reddit account-aging and community judgment.** `16_community_playbook.md` says "build the account before you launch the brand" — this requires having lurked these subs for months already. A cold-start Reddit account in week 1 is automod-shadowbanned by default.
4. **Cold-pitch outreach to creators, journalists, retail buyers.** Tier-2 podcast booking, Substack-writer warm intros, retail-buyer scout — all assume founder-rolodex or DM-comfort skills.
5. **Helium 10 / Amazon listing optimization fluency.** `19_amazon_playbook.md` references Cerebro reverse-ASIN, Black Box, Brand Tailored Promotions, ACoS pacing — these are Amazon-seller-operator skills that take 3–6 months of tooling apprenticeship.
6. **Klaviyo flow setup, SMS welcome flows, attribution tracking.** Email/SMS automation tooling fluency is assumed throughout `21_speed_to_100.md`.
7. **Spoken-Thai or warm Thai-American social network.** `15_tiktok_playbook.md` Tier-3 micro-creator strategy requires the founder write a "handwritten Thai note" with each box. Cultural-credibility shielding requires more than Google Translate.
8. **Photography / iPhone product styling for PDPs and A+ Content.** Hero packshot + use-occasion grid + supplier-on-camera videography all assumed.

## 4. What to cut (50% plan)

If the founder can only do half of this realistically, keep the channels with **highest signal-per-hour and zero-or-negative replication risk** — drop the ones that compound slowly or have no distribution moat.

**KEEP (35–45 hr/wk realistic):**
- **IRL sampling — 1 city, 2 venues, 6 hr/wk.** Highest ROI per dollar; the only channel where a sensory product converts properly. The whole brand fails without it.
- **Founder LinkedIn — 2 posts/week, 4 hr/wk.** Buyer/journalist/investor channel; high optionality.
- **TikTok founder POV — 4 posts/week (batch-shot), 6 hr/wk.** The trial engine. Can't skip but can de-cadence.
- **Amazon listing prep + trademark filing — 4 hr/wk.** Trademark is a cost-of-doing-business one-time; listing draft sits and waits for Brand Registry.
- **Friend DMs + customer interviews — 3 hr/wk.** Free, highest-quality early signal.
- **OEM ops + fulfillment + CS — 8 hr/wk.** Non-negotiable.
- **One Reddit anchor post per month + r/aromatherapy lurk — 2 hr/wk.** Compound slowly but cheaply.

**CUT or DEFER to month 4+:**
- **SEO pillar content (5 articles in 30 days)** — defer. SEO is 9–12 month payoff anyway; ship 1 article in 90 days, not 5.
- **Substack essay biweekly** — defer to month 4. LinkedIn covers the journalist signal at half the cost.
- **8-community Reddit + Discord + Substack outreach** — collapse to r/aromatherapy only.
- **10-podcast ladder** — defer. Tier-1 alone in months 4–6.
- **Creator seeding at 50–80 creators** — collapse to 5–10 hand-picked; volume seeding is a team task.
- **Farmers markets + co-working + pop-ups** — drop. Tier-1 coffee shops + 1 yoga studio only.
- **Daily X reply-thread** — drop. Zero customer-acquisition impact in 90 days.

This brings the plan to **38–48 founder-hours/week** — still high but compatible with a 50-hour week if the founder has no day job.

## 5. Verdict

**Requires team — minimum 1 part-time contractor + 1 fractional specialist.**

Solo execution of the full plan is **not feasible** in 90 days for a human with a circulatory system. The 71–90 hr/wk total is a structural impossibility, and the "cut to 50%" version still demands 38–48 hr/wk plus skills (video editing, SEO writing, Amazon ops, Klaviyo automation, scent-vocabulary articulation, Thai-cultural network) that no single founder reliably has.

**Minimum viable team for the published 90-day plan:**
1. **Founder (40 hr/wk):** in-person sampling, LinkedIn writing, supplier relationships, customer interviews, OEM ops, decision-making.
2. **Part-time content contractor (~10 hr/wk @ $25–40/hr = $1,000–1,600/mo):** TikTok edit, Reddit drafting, SEO articles, Klaviyo flows. Pulls 12–15 hr/wk of work off the founder.
3. **Fractional Amazon specialist (~$500–1,500/mo):** Helium 10 ops, listing optimization, PPC pacing. Removes 5–7 hr/wk and a steep learning curve.

Annualized contractor cost: $18k–37k, which eats 35–75% of the $50k Y1 budget that v5 allocates entirely to inventory, sampling, and paid media. **The plan as written cannot fund the team it secretly requires.**

**Honest recommendation**: Either reduce the 90-day customer target from 100 to 40 (solo-feasible at 40 hr/wk on a half-plan), OR raise the Y1 budget to $75k to fund 1 part-time contractor + the marketing spend, OR explicitly delay the 100-customer milestone to day 150.
