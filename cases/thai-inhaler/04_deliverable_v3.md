## 2. The 60-Second Reasoning

**Custom inventory before validation is the single most expensive mistake available to you.** A four-link conjunctive bet (vocabulary holds + bundle converts + cosmetic-language premium-prices + bootstrap beats Plant Therapy's decade of Amazon SEO) at 70% per link = ~24% joint probability. That is "design hypothesis worth a $5k test," not "commit $25–100k."

- **Counter-argument: "First-mover matters."** It does — but Apothékary already has $8M Shiseido pre-Series A and is one format-pivot from your wedge. You do not out-race them; you out-narrate them. Heritage moat > format moat.
- **Counter-argument: "$5k won't tell you anything."** Correct — directionally it tells you "definitely below kill" or "definitely above strong-go." For 95% CI on the conditional band, budget $10–15k. Do not pretend $5k buys statistical confidence.
- **The wedge is borderline-real white space**, not fictional white space. Empty for executable reasons (FDA language + cultural authenticity + bundle economics), not because demand is null.
- **Median performance is not validation.** Indie-wellness median conversion is 1.5%. Your wedge thesis claims to *beat* median. Clearing it is a yellow light, not green.

## 4. Phase 0: Validate Cheaply ($5–15k, 6 weeks)

**Run all four tests in parallel; the decision gate is a 4-signal AND.**

- **Private-label MVP ($5k base / $10–15k for statistical power).** 500 Poy-Sian units + custom over-sleeve, listed at $14/$24/$36 on Amazon + Shopify, $1.5–3k Meta ads.
- **Fake-PDP price test ($3k).** $9.99 / $14.99 / $19.99 with 3 creative variants per price; measure CTR + add-to-cart drop-off.
- **FDA language audit ($2k).** 1–2 hours of cosmetic-FDA counsel review on website headline, top 5 PDP claims, affiliate brief.
- **Creator gifting.** 30 nervous-system + Thai-American micro-creators. Free; signal is pickup rate.

**Decision gate (all thresholds recalibrated above indie-wellness median):**

| Metric | Kill | Conditional | Strong-go |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cold-ad conversion at $14 | <1.0% | 1.5–2.5% | >2.5% |
| AOV | <$18 | $18–22 | ≥$22 |
| 30-day return rate | >8% | 5–8% | <5% |
| Creator pickup (of 30 gifted) | <10% | 10–20% | ≥20% |
| Blended CAC | >$25 | $20–25 | <$20 |
| Subscription opt-in | <5% | 5–8% | ≥8% |
| Compliance events | Any | — | Zero |

**Rule:** Strong-go requires ≥3-of-4 primary signals at strong-go, no metric in kill, zero compliance flags. Anything less = pivot to Adjacent Wedge B (bundle toolkit) or audience-first content path.

## 5. Phase 1: Launch If Validated

**Amazon-first, one Erewhon door, no chain retail. $50k allocation, inventory separate.**

- **Channel sequence.** Amazon launch ($14k: listing + Vine + 90-day PPC on 8 keywords). DTC PDP live, no spend. One Erewhon door by month 4 ($6k including 12 demos) — PR ROI, not revenue ROI.
- **Pricing.** $14 / $24 / $36 with 15%-off subscription on the 2-pack. Subscription is the only configuration where DTC paid CAC math closes.
- **Creators ($9k).** Nervous-system educators (clinical-safe briefs), Thai-American micro-creators 5K–50K, sensory-ritual creators. Hard exclude: biohackers, nootropic creators, anyone with prior Young Living / doTERRA / Hong Thai sponsorship.
- **Compliance.** Tape the verb-object rule to your monitor (see `04_details/04_fda_compliance.md`). Brief every creator.
- **Founder narrative on day one.** Named Thai supplier with photo, public producer-pricing disclosure, founder origin essay. All six elements of the credible-carrier playbook live before launch.

## 6. Kill-Switches

Pin these to the wall before Phase 0 starts.

- **Conversion <1.0% at $14** → pivot to Adjacent Wedge B.
- **Creative fatigue (3 variants × 4 weeks miss threshold)** → messaging fail; pivot frame before product.
- **No supplier LOI by day 60** → activate Plan B parallel track immediately.
- **Any compliance event in Phase 0** → automatic full-stop, restart after fix.
- **Apothékary or Saje launches inhaler-format SKU** → halt cold-acquisition, accelerate retention loop, capture LTV.
- **FDA warning letter to comparable indie within 6 months** → tighten language proactively, second hour of counsel.
